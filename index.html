About Our Clinic

Dr. Aaron is a spine surgeon specializing in traumatology and orthopedic surgery, with extensive experience, based in Extremadura. He has received training at prestigious centers of excellence in spine surgery in Spain, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

He specializes in the entire spectrum of spine problems, including sciatica, back pain (lumbago), neck pain, childhood and adult deformities (scoliosis), trauma, infections, and tumor surgery.
